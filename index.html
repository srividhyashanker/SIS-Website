Our Mission

Welcome to UC Berkeley South Indian Society’s Website! Our club was initiated in April of 2016. We have successfully launched several events including Southside, our first dance party, and Dosas and Mimosas, our formal banquet event.

We’re very excited for the future of our club. If you are interested in joining as a member or have any general inquiries, please contact sisberkeley@gmail.com.
